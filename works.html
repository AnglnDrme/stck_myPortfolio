PICTURES




Cartoon 1

I made this work way back 2020 wherein I have a plety of time to spare because of the pandemic. I saw some people changing their Facebook profiles to a "Cartoon" version of themselves. Due to curiosity, my creative mind pushed me to follow the trend that time. By the time I'm making this, I'm still new to Adobe Photoshop so it took me hours to finish this work.



Cartoon 2

This was made right after I finish my first work. I tried to put additional details to this work such as facial features. I tried to add a shadow to this work but unfortunately, I'm still not good at editing on Photoshop.



Cartoon 3

This work is one of my least favorites but I'm still proud of what I've done since it shows my progress on my editing skills. I added shadows to this work this time unlike the first two works that I made.



Cartoon 4

When the time pandemic had just begun, people are not allowed to go outside because of the quarantine. Since then I'm always in my home, I always reminiscing about the times where I can freely spend my time with my friends. Our trip in National Museum way back 2018 was the first thing popped in my head that time. So I decided to make this artwork to relive that moment.



Cartoon 5

As I remiscize the past, I missed hanging out with my friends. And among the list of friends that I have, there is one person that I've been friends with for a very long time. We've met in high school and were become friends ever since. So I decide to make this work back then because I really miss hanging out with my friends, especially my bestfriend.



Cartoon 6

I remember that it has been a year after I graduated senior high school when I made this. As I remember every ups and downs that I need to take in order for me to reach what I am now. So I decided to make this work to tell my future self that no matter how difficult her situation now, she can finish them just as how she overcome the problems she faced in the past.



Cartoon 7

I made this work as a gift for my bestfriend on her birthday last 2020. I have nothing to give her that time so I decided to make a cartoon version of her Facebook profile back then. As I see this work, I realize that this is my best works so far. It shows my progress from zero knowledge to beginner to intermidiatte level of editing in photoshop.
