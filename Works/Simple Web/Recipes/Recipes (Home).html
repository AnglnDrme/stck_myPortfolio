Local Dishes

Beef Bulalo
A beef soup comprised of shank with bone marrow still inside the bone, Bulalo is considered to be one of the most favorite main dishes in the Philippines.
Beef Kaldereta
A main stay in any Filipino Kitchen. It is a type of beef stew cooked with tomato sauce and liver spread.
Chicken Adobo
A type of Filipino chicken stew. Chicken pieces are marinated in soy sauce and spices, pan-fried, and stewed until tender.
Chicken Tinola
A Filipino soup dish. It involves cooking chicken pieces in ginger broth. It is a popular cold weather dish.
Kare-Kare
A type of Filipino stew with a rich and thick peanut sauce. It is a popular dish in the Philippines served during special occasions.
Lechon Kawali
A crispy deep-fried pork is the simplest way to describe lechon kawali. Pork belly or liempo is the cut of pork usually used to cook it.
Pinakbet
A Tagalog Filipino vegetable dish. It is composed of a variety of vegetables and it also has a protein component.
Pork Nilaga
A type of Filipino pork soup in clear broth. I used pork belly in this version along with other ingredients such as potato, saba banana, and pechay (or baby bok choy).
Pork Sinigang
A sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient.
Pork Sisig
A popular Filipino dish. It can be considered as a main dish or an appetizer. The composition of this dish is mainly of pig’s parts such as minced pork meat, ears, and face.

International Dishes

Beef Wellington
A steak dish of English origin, made out of fillet steak coated with pâté and duxelles, wrapped in puff pastry, then baked.
Chili Con Carne
Also spelled chilli con carne or chile con carne, meaning "chili with meat", is a spicy stew containing chili peppers, meat, tomatoes and kidney beans.
Clam Chowder
Any of several chowder soups in American cuisine containing clams.
Jambalaya
A creole rice dish of West African, French, and Spanish influence, consisting mainly of meat and vegetables mixed with rice.
Mac and Cheese
Also called macaroni and cheese and macaroni cheese is a dish of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar.
Pork Roast
This classic roast pork recipe with lots of delicious crackling is great for Sunday lunch with the family. Top with our tasty apple and cider gravy.
Raveoli
A type of pasta comprising a filling enveloped in thin pasta dough. Usually served in broth or with a sauce, they originated as a traditional food in Italian cuisine.
Risotto
A northern Italian rice dish cooked with broth until it reaches a creamy consistency. The broth can be derived from meat, fish, or vegetables.
Shepherd's Pie
Also reffered as cottage pie, or hachis Parmentier is a ground meat pie with a crust or topping of mashed potato; It is most likely of English origin.
Vegetable Stew
A combination of solid food ingredients that have been cooked in liquid and served in the resultant gravy.
